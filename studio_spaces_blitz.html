Studios

Blitz Gallery also has 4 artist studio spaces for rent. Studios are subsidised by Arts Council Ireland. While it is open to artists of all disciplines to apply we do endeavour to nuture those in the early stages of their careers. If you wish to apply for a space or simply find out more please fill out a contact form here. Or email us at this address: info@blitzgallery.com.
